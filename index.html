ABOUT

Cause Unknown is an independant solo metal project based out of Austin, Texas. Instrumental metal ranging from death to progressive and various subgenres inbetween. All composition, guitar/bass playing, drum programming, and production done by David Menendez. The first official album Ghost Metamorphosis is expected to be released by fall of 2020.
